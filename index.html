About

I am a Master's student in Industrial Mathematics and Data Analysis at the University of Bremen with a strong interest in Artificial Intelligence and data-driven business processes.

Currently, I work as a Student Research Assistant in collaboration with the University of Bremen and the German Aerospace Center (DLR), where I support an applied modeling project involving data preparation, numerical simulation, and visualization in Python.
